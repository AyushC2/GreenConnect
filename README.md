 GreenConnect

GreenConnect is a smart and sustainable platform designed to promote eco-friendly practices by connecting individuals, communities, and organizations.
It helps users track, manage, and contribute to environmental initiatives such as waste management, recycling, and green living.

Features
♻️ Waste Management System
Smart tracking and categorization of waste for better recycling.
🌍 Eco Awareness
Provides tips and insights for sustainable living.
📊 Data Insights & Analytics
Visual representation of environmental impact and user contributions.
🤝 Community Engagement
Connect with like-minded individuals and participate in eco-events.
🔔 Smart Notifications
Reminders and alerts for eco-friendly activities.
